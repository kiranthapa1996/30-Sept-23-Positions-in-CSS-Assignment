The Artificial Satellites

An artificial satellite is a human made object which orbits around the earth and other planets in the solar system. Artificial satellites are built in order to study the earth and other planets, to help in communication and to observe the distance universe. Satellites can also have people in them just like International Space stations. Soviet Sputnik 1 mission was the first artificial satellite which was launched in 1957. After that many countries have launched many satellites and there are around 3000 satellites orbiting the earth currently. It is estimated that there are eight thousand pieces of space junk and the dead satellites which are also going around the earth. The satellites are launched into various orbits depending upon their function.
